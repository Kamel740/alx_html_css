EGYPT

Ancient Egypt history

Ancient Egypt was one of the world’s first great civilizations, known for its incredible achievements in architecture, art, and science. It began around 3100 BCE and lasted for over 3,000 years.

Pyramids of Giza

The pyramids of Giza are among the most famous monuments in the world. The Great Pyramid of Khufu was originally 146 meters tall and was the tallest human-made structure for over 3,800 years.

believes

Egyptians believed strongly in the afterlife. That’s why they mummified their dead and buried them with food, jewelry, and other goods they might need in the next life.

writing and reading

Hieroglyphics, the writing system of ancient Egypt, combined pictures and symbols to tell stories and record history. It was used in temples, tombs, and even on jewelry.
